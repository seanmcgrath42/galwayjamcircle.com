Galway Jam Circle

An easy-going, mostly acoustic jam, playing all sorts of songs from Folk to Rock to Blues to Americana and beyond.

When & Where

When
Most Saturdays, 4pm – 7pm — check socials
Where
Áras na nGael, Dominic Street, Galway

About

The Galway Jam Circle is a weekly gathering of musicians who come together to play songs in a relaxed, informal setting. The circle is mostly acoustic and covers a wide range of styles — Folk, Rock, Blues, Americana, and even a touch of Jazz from time to time.

How It Works

We use the Let's Jam Method, specifically designed to facilitate community-based jam circles. It brings beginners, intermediate, and advanced musicians together in a relaxed, inclusive setting.

The Let's Jam Family

Galway Jam Circle is a founding member of Let's Jam, a not-for-profit organisation dedicated to fostering inclusive jam circles in communities in Ireland and beyond.
